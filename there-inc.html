There Inc.
3D Modeler/Texture Artist  June 2003 - May 2004

Contributed to this Social MMO startup that also featured some gaming elements and user content for about a year until the company disbanded in the Spring of 2004.  Created a variety of environments and props for this live service including new settings like "The Cannery" and tie-ins with other web presences like iVillage and Astrology.com.  Created a multitude of objects to be sold in conjunction with promotions, content updates, holidays, etc., as well as building blocks for users to create/design their own houses/personal spaces.  Created character hair/clothing/accessory variants that were modular in nature to be used with any character and deformable within set limits much like morph-targets.

Platforms: PC
Current Relaunched Site:  www.there.com
Cannery/waterfront environment used for a CD cover for a new release of the product.
